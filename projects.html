Some Projects I have completed


Huffman Codec

A C project for Huffman encoding/decoding. This works by couning the frequency of various characters in a training file that is used to establish typical frequencies of these characters. Next, you use these frequencies to build a Huffman tree in memory. By walking the tree, you then find a Huffman encoding for each of 256 characters in the ASCII character set. Then you can finally encode/decode files.
